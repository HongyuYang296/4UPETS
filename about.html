ABOUT THE CAFE

4upets start from the late summer of 2022, our team motivated by shared goals: To raise the general awareness of dogs' health during the heatwave. To provide useful and heart-warming tips for you to better look after your dogs during the heatwave To help you deal with dogs related affairs by "Functions" 4upets is a good facilitator for those of you who have difficulty helping your dogs suffering during the heatwave
